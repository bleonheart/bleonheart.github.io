<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Comprehensive banking system with multiple accounts, deposits, withdrawals, transfers, item storage, check writing and redemption, ATM integration, and paycheck system for managing player finances and assets.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- Multiple Accounts Up to 5 accounts per player with configurable pricing per account slot
- VIP Accounts Accounts 4 and 5 require VIP status to create
- Account Members Add members with customizable permissions for deposits, withdrawals, and transfers
- Account Renaming Rename accounts with custom names (max 50 characters)
- Item Storage 10x10 regular and 16x16 VIP inventory storage per account (configurable sizes)
- Check System Seven check designs (standard, liberty blue, liberty painted, green, red, gold, purple) with faction restrictions
- Check Writing Write checks with custom amounts and recipient information
- Check Redemption Redeem checks at ATMs or directly to funds
- Interest System 5% interest every 7 days with maximum cap of 10000 (configurable rate and period)
- ATM Integration Automated teller machines for deposits, withdrawals, transfers, and check redemption
- Paycheck Integration Paychecks automatically deposit into accounts or can be withdrawn manually
- Admin Panel View and manage all player accounts, freeze accounts, and override restrictions
- Account Deletion Players can request account deletion (admin approval may be required)
