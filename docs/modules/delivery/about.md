<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Parcel delivery side-job system providing players with legitimate income opportunities through courier work. Provides features such as offers parcel delivery side jobs, random drop-off locations per mission, pays players money on completion, NPC shows delivery progress via dialog, and encourages exploration of the map.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- Delivery Jobs Accept delivery missions from NPCs
- Delivery Crates Spawn crates that must be delivered to locations
- Waypoint System Automatic waypoints to delivery destinations
- Time Sensitive Optional time limits with bonus rewards
- Delivery Trucks Special vehicles for delivery work
- Faction System Join delivery faction to work as courier
- Payment System Earn money for completing deliveries
- Multiple Locations Deliver to various locations across map
