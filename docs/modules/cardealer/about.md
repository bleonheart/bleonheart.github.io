<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Comprehensive vehicle purchasing and management system allowing players to buy, customize, repair, and store vehicles through specialized car dealer NPCs. Features custom paint jobs, bodygroup modifications, garage storage, and integration with driving addons.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- Vehicle Purchase Buy vehicles from dealer NPC with configurable pricing
- Vehicle Return Return owned vehicles for refund
- Repair Service Repair damaged vehicles for a fee
- Paint Jobs Customize vehicle colors and finishes with configurable pricing
- Bodygroups Modify vehicle bodygroups and parts
- Garage Storage Store vehicles safely in garages
- Addon Support Works seamlessly with driving addons
