<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Vehicle quality of life features including CD player system with music tracks, albums, and audio streaming for in-car entertainment. Provides enhanced vehicle interaction and customization options for improved gameplay experience.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- CD Player System Play music from CD items in inventory while in vehicle
- Music Tracks Select specific tracks from albums with multiple songs
- Audio Streaming Stream audio content for in-car entertainment
- Vehicle Locking Toggle lock and unlock from inside vehicle
- Trunk Access Open vehicle trunk storage inventory
- Kick Passengers Remove passengers from vehicle via dropdown menu
- Owner Driver Only owner or driver can use these features
