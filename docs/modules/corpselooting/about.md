<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Corpse looting system allowing players to search dead bodies, transfer items and money, with configurable search distance and anti-spam protection.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- Corpse Search Open inventory of dead players to transfer items
- Money Transfer Take money from corpses with configurable amounts
- Distance Limit Maximum 80 unit distance to interact with corpses
- Anti-Spam Protection Rate limiting prevents repeated looting attempts
- Item Transfer Drag and drop items between corpse and player inventory
- Money Options Deposit or withdraw money from corpse inventory
