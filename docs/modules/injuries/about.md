<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Adds in-depth damage to legs with side effects. Advanced medical injury system featuring broken leg mechanics, bleeding variants, concussion effects, pain systems, weapon-based injury chances, fall damage integration, surgical kit healing, and comprehensive medical weapon support for immersive roleplay scenarios.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- Four Injury Types Broken leg, bleeding, concussion, and pain
- Broken Leg 50% speed reduction triggered by leg hits
- Bleeding Variants Light and heavy bleeding with damage over time
- Auto Heal Bleeding heals automatically after duration timer
- Concussion Screen effects sent via network message
- Pain 80% speed reduction until health at 100 or drug taken
- Weapon Chances Different weapons have different injury chances
- Fall Damage Falling can trigger broken leg with 50% chance
- Surgical Kits Use surgical kit items to heal injuries via minigame
- Character Storage Injuries saved to character data
- Minigame System Interactive healing minigame for surgical procedures
