<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

The Black Market NPC module implements an underground contraband trading system that allows players to purchase illegal weapons, items, and contraband through hidden black market dealers. Players must locate secret black market NPCs and can place orders for various illicit goods with realistic import delays, risk mechanics, and delivery systems that encourage exploration and underground roleplay scenarios.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- Hidden NPCs Black market NPCs teleport between locations
- Basket Orders Buy multiple items in one order
- Import Delay Items arrive after configurable delay timer
- Random Drops Items spawn at random locations or player position
- Cooldown System Prevents repeated orders with cooldown timer
- Waypoint System Automatic waypoint to package location
- Package Expiration Packages despawn after time limit
- Proximity Check Must be near NPC to place orders
