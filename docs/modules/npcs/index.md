<h1 style="text-align:center; font-size:2rem; font-weight:bold;">About</h1>

Roleplay NPC framework with dialogue trees, quest systems, and submodule support (mechanics, taxis, vendors, fences) with centralized NPC management and customization tools.

<h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:1.5rem; font-weight:bold;">Main Features</h2>

- NPC Framework Modular system for custom NPCs
- Dialogue Trees Branching conversation systems
- Quest System NPCs provide and track quests
- Easy Creation Simple configuration for new NPCs
- Centralized Management Unified NPC script system
- Vehicle Dealers Car purchasing and garage management
- Property Management Real estate and door ownership
- Repair Services Mechanic NPCs for vehicle repair
- Black Market Underground economy NPCs
- Delivery System Courier NPCs with waypoints
